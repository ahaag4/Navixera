Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Smart Tracker GPS?
Our GPS tracking is accurate within 5 meters under normal conditions. In urban areas with tall buildings, accuracy might be slightly reduced, but we use multiple technologies to ensure the best possible tracking precision.
How long does the battery last on your tracking devices?
Battery life varies by device. Our standard vehicle tracker lasts up to 2 weeks on a single charge with normal use. Our premium models can last up to 30 days. For asset tracking, we have devices that can last up to 6 months.
Can I track multiple vehicles with one account?
Yes, our business plans allow you to track multiple vehicles from a single dashboard. You can create groups, set up geofences for each vehicle, and receive individual alerts.
Is my tracking data secure?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all location data and follow industry best practices for data security. Your tracking information is only accessible to you and those you explicitly authorize.
